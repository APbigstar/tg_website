About TG Games

A social gaming & rewards platform, driven by community participation, enabling players from around the world to engage in telegram-based games & tournaments, offering tangible rewards to forge meaningful connections.

  • Stake-and-play-to-earn social tournaments to foster social connections & Sponsored tournaments allowing new revenue generation.
